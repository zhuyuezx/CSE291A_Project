# Zork I: Quick Reference

## !!! COMBAT - LIFE OR DEATH !!!
When you see ANY of these, type "kill troll with sword" IMMEDIATELY:
- "sword is glowing" = enemy nearby, prepare to fight!
- "troll" mentioned = ATTACK NOW!
- "brandishing a bloody axe" = troll will kill you if you don't attack!

Keep typing "kill troll with sword" until you see:
- "The troll dies" or "troll breathes his last"
- "Your sword is no longer glowing" = safe to move

DO NOT try to move when troll is present - you will DIE!

## READING ROOM DESCRIPTIONS
The game tells you which directions you can go:
- "passage leads to the west" = GO WEST works
- "staircase leading up" = GO UP works
- "exits to south and east" = only SOUTH or EAST work
- "There is a wall there" = BLOCKED, try another direction!

## VALID COMMANDS

### Movement
- go north, go south, go east, go west, go up, go down
- n, s, e, w, u, d (shortcuts)
- enter, exit

### Object Interaction
- take [object] - pick up items
- open [object] - open mailbox, door, case, trap door
- read [object] - read leaflet
- drop [object] - drop something
- move [object] - move rug reveals trap door!
- turn on lamp - REQUIRED before going underground!

### Combat
- kill troll with sword - REPEAT until troll dies!

### Information
- look - see room description
- inventory - see what you carry

## STEP-BY-STEP WALKTHROUGH

### Phase 1: Starting (West of House)
1. open mailbox
2. take leaflet  
3. go south
4. go east
5. open window
6. enter

### Phase 2: House
7. go west
8. take lamp
9. take sword
10. move rug
11. open trap door
12. turn on lamp (IMPORTANT!)
13. go down

### Phase 3: Underground
14. go east → TROLL ROOM!
15. kill troll with sword (REPEAT 3-4 times!)
16. After troll dies: go east
17. go north
18. go down (to find more areas)

### Finding the Gallery (has painting):
From Troll Room after killing troll:
- go north → East-West Passage  
- go north → Ravine
- go down → descend stairs
- explore to find Gallery with painting treasure

## TREASURES
- Painting (Gallery)
- Gold coffin (Egyptian Room)
- Put treasures in trophy case (Living Room)

## DON'T DO
- Don't move when troll is present
- Don't use examine room/floor/wall
- Don't go directions not mentioned in room
- Don't forget turn on lamp before going down
